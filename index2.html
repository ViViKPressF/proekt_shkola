Apple Pie
classic British dessert

Ingredients (8 servings)

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3-4 apples
  • Vanillin or vanilla sugar to taste
  • A pinch of baking soda

Instructions

  • Prepare classic Charlotte dough. Whisk eggs and sugar until light yellow and fluffy.
  • Add sifted flour with soda and vanilla. Whisk again. The dough is ready.
  • Wash and cut apples in half. Remove cores. Peel and cut into small cubes or slices.
  • Add apples to the dough and mix gently. Pour into a greased mold. Bake at 190°C for 30 minutes.
🍏 Serve warm with custard or vanilla ice cream.
Fruit Fool
light fruit dessert

Ingredients

  • 300g mixed berries
  • 50g sugar
  • 250ml double cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  • Pure half of the berries with sugar until smooth.
  • Whip the cream with vanilla until soft peaks form.
  • Gently fold the berry purée into the whipped cream.
  • Spoon into glasses, layer with remaining whole berries.
  • Chill for at least 1 hour before serving. Garnish with mint.
💡 Tip: Use fresh seasonal berries for the best flavor.
Bara Brith
Welsh fruit loaf

Ingredients

  • 450g mixed dried fruit
  • 300ml strong hot tea
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 100g light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp mixed spice

Instructions

  • Soak dried fruit in hot tea overnight.
  • Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a loaf tin.
  • Stir flour, sugar, spice and beaten egg into the fruit mixture.
  • Pour into the tin and bake for 1.5 hours.
  • Cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn out.
🍞 Serve sliced with butter and Welsh tea.
