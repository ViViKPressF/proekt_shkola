Fruit Fool
light fruit dessert

Ingredients

  • 300g mixed berries
  • 50g sugar
  • 250ml double cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  • Pure half of the berries with sugar until smooth.
  • Whip the cream with vanilla until soft peaks form.
  • Gently fold the berry purée into the whipped cream.
  • Spoon into glasses, layer with remaining whole berries.
  • Chill for at least 1 hour before serving. Garnish with mint.
💡 Tip: Use fresh seasonal berries for the best flavor.
Welsh Rarebit
cheesy toast with a twist

Ingredients

  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 200g mature cheddar
  • 2 tbsp ale or milk
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  • Preheat grill to medium-high.
  • Mix cheese, ale, mustard, egg yolk and Worcestershire sauce.
  • Toast bread lightly on both sides.
  • Spread cheese mixture over each slice.
  • Grill until bubbling and golden brown (3-4 minutes).
🍸 Serve with a pint of ale for an authentic British experience.
Apple Pie
classic British dessert

Ingredients

  • Pastry: 250g flour, 125g butter, salt, water
  • 800g apples (Bramley or Granny Smith)
  • 100g sugar + extra
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg for egg wash

Instructions

  • Make pastry: rub butter into flour. Add water, form dough. Chill 30 min.
  • Peel, core and slice apples. Mix with sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice.
  • Roll out pastry for base. Line a pie dish.
  • Fill with apples. Cover with pastry lid, seal edges.
  • Brush with egg wash, sprinkle sugar. Bake at 200°C for 40-45 min.
🍏 Serve warm with custard or vanilla ice cream.
