Welsh Cakes
traditional Welsh griddle cakes

Ingredients

  • 225g plain flour
  • 100g cold butter, cubed
  • 85g caster sugar
  • 75g currants or raisins
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp mixed spice
  • Milk if needed

Instructions

  • Rub butter into flour until breadcrumb-like.
  • Stir in sugar, currants and mixed spice.
  • Add egg and mix to form dough. Add milk if too dry.
  • Roll out to 5mm thickness. Cut into rounds.
  • Cook on a greased griddle for 3-4 minutes each side until golden.
  • Sprinkle with caster sugar while warm.
🔥 Best enjoyed warm with a cup of tea.
Cawl
traditional Welsh lamb stew

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb, cubed
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 leeks, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 swede, diced
  • 1.5 liters stock
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions

  • Brown the lamb in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  • Add all vegetables except potatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Pour in stock, bring to boil, then simmer for 1.5 hours.
  • Add potatoes and cook for another 30 minutes until tender.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley.
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁷󠁬󠁳󠁿 Serve with crusty bread and Caerphilly cheese.
Welsh Rarebit
cheesy toast with a twist

Ingredients

  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 200g mature cheddar
  • 2 tbsp ale or milk
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  • Preheat grill to medium-high.
  • Mix cheese, ale, mustard, egg yolk and Worcestershire sauce.
  • Toast bread lightly on both sides.
  • Spread cheese mixture over each slice.
  • Grill until bubbling and golden brown (3-4 minutes).
🍸 Serve with a pint of ale for an authentic British experience.
